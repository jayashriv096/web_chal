Our story

Two ovens, one dream.

Sweet Haven started in a rented kitchen with two ovens and a stack of handwritten recipes passed down from a grandmother who never once measured flour by the cup.

Where we began

In 2014, Sweet Haven opened its doors as a single counter bakery. What started as weekend baking for friends turned into a full-time shop after one too many people asked, "can I actually buy this?"

Our values

Real butter, real sugar, and no shortcuts on proofing time.

Our team

A crew of six bakers who all agree that fondant is overrated.

Our promise

If it isn't good enough to serve our own families, it doesn't leave the kitchen.
